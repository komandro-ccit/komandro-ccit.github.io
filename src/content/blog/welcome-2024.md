Welcome to Komandro 2024! We're excited to start another year of learning, building, and growing together.

## What's New This Year

This year, we're expanding our curriculum to include:

- **Advanced Android Development**: Jetpack Compose and modern architecture patterns
- **Backend Systems**: Node.js, Python, and Go
- **Cloud Computing**: AWS and Google Cloud Platform
- **Cyber Security**: Ethical hacking and security best practices

## How to Join

Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, there's a place for you in Komandro. Here's how you can get started:

1. Attend our weekly meetups every Thursday at 4 PM
2. Join our Discord community
3. Follow us on Instagram for updates

## Upcoming Events

- **Hackathon**: March 15-17
